Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How can I place an order?

You can place an order through our website by adding products to your cart and proceeding to checkout. Alternatively, you can call our customer service at +91 7013384658 to place an order over the phone.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery for eligible orders.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services across India. Delivery times typically range from 2-5 business days depending on your location. For bulk orders, please contact us for special delivery arrangements.

How do I know which product is right for my crops?

Our team of agricultural experts can help you determine the right products for your specific crops and soil conditions. You can contact us through the form above or call our customer service for personalized recommendations.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk orders. The discount percentage varies based on the quantity and products ordered. Please contact our sales team for more information on bulk pricing.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the product is in its original packaging and unused condition. Please note that certain products like opened fertilizers cannot be returned due to safety and quality concerns.
